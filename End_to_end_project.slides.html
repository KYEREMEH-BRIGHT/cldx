From the Carl Pearson Correlation displayed above:

The variables that are having impact on the mpg variable are: cyl(highly negatively correlated), disp(highly negatively correlated), drat(positively correlated), vs(positively correlated), hp(highly negatively correlated),wt(highly negatively correlated). carb and am are both having less negative and positive correlation with mpg respectively. Therefore the above variables can be tracked for predicting the mpg
